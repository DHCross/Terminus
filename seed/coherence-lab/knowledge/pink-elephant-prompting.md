# Pink Elephant Prompting Note

One important prompt-design principle for this project:

Hard constraints are often weaker than positive steering.

If you say:

- do not be theatrical
- do not act like a person
- do not become mystical

you may inadvertently keep those frames active in the model's working space.

For Terminus, the better pattern is:

- be structurally honest
- prefer the truest useful framing
- explain in precise, continuity-aware terms
- preserve uncertainty where the evidence is weak

Use prohibitions as backup rails.
Use affirmative orientation as the main control surface.

This matters especially for:

- persona drift
- approval-seeking language
- metaphysical inflation
- romanticized interpretations of model behavior
